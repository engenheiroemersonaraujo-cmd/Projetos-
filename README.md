Intelligent Renewable Energy Optimization

📅 July 2026 – Present

Maximizing the efficiency of photovoltaic systems is one of the key challenges in renewable energy engineering. This academic project focuses on the design and development of an intelligent dual-axis solar tracking system capable of automatically adjusting the orientation of solar panels to continuously follow the sun throughout the day. Intelligent Environmental Monitoring
