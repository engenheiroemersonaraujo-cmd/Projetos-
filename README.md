### Energy-Efficient Urban Infrastructure

## 🌍 IoT-Based Smart Air Quality Monitoring System
### Intelligent Environmental Monitoring

📅 **June 2026 – Present**

Monitoring air quality has become increasingly important for protecting public health and supporting sustainable urban development. This academic project explores the development of an Internet of Things (IoT)-based environmental monitoring system capable of measuring air quality parameters through integrated sensors and transmitting real-time data to cloud platforms for analysis and visualization. The project combines embedded electronics, wireless communication, cloud computing, and data analytics to create intelligent environmental monitoring solutions.

### 🔬 Areas of Knowledge

## ☀️ Smart Dual-Axis Solar Tracking System
### Intelligent Renewable Energy Optimization

📅 **July 2026 – Present**

Maximizing the efficiency of photovoltaic systems is one of the key challenges in renewable energy engineering.
